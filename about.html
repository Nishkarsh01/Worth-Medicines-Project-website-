About Us

At Worth, we constantly work towards ensuring access to high quality and affordable medicines to support patients in need. Which is why, we have been trusted by health care professionals and patients across geographies for the last 15 years.

Heritage

Worth Medicines came into existence in the year of 2005, under the able leadership of Mr. Manoj Chaudhary (Director at Worth Medicines India Pvt Ltd). Today, we are one of the leading Indian pharma companies and strive to provide world-class medications at affordable prices.
